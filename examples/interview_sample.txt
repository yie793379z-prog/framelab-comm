[Interview Excerpt 1 | Student journalist | English]
When the university announced the attendance policy update, the official email sounded neutral, but students in our newsroom immediately framed it as a trust issue. People were less interested in the policy details than in why the decision was made without student consultation.

[访谈片段 2 | 研究生 | 中文]
我觉得学校公众号的语气一直很“通知式”，信息很完整，但没有回应学生为什么会焦虑。很多同学转发的时候都会自己加一句解释，好像必须重新包装一下，内容才更像是在和人说话。

[Interview Excerpt 3 | Community media volunteer | English]
In our training sessions, we tell new volunteers not to treat every interview as a quote-hunting exercise. Sometimes the first useful code is not a theme but a role: resident, organizer, local official, or platform moderator. That changes how you read the same sentence.

[访谈片段 4 | 新闻编辑 | 中文]
如果只是为了课堂作业，我会建议学生先做一轮预编码，把“问题如何被定义”“谁在发声”“有没有明确行动建议”这些内容先标出来。这样后面做主题归纳时，心里会更有结构。
