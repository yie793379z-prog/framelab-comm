[Statement 1 | English]
Northline Water apologizes for the service outage that affected three neighborhoods on Tuesday evening. Our initial update failed to explain how long repairs might take, and that uncertainty increased frustration for residents. We have opened a temporary help desk, waived late-payment penalties for the week, and commissioned an external review of emergency notification timing.
